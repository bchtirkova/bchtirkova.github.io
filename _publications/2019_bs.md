We investigated Argo float data from the Black Sea and showed that cold water formation events are becoming weaker with
warmer winters. The cold intermediate layer, a prominent feature of the Black Sea, is disappearing.

The figure shows a Hovmöller diagram of temperature in density coordinates with three distinct cold water formation
events, which could not sufficiently refill the cold intermediate layer.
