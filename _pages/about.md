I am a Postdoctoral fellow at Princeton University. My research focuses on the repartitioning of energy fluxes through the climate system and how this affects the planetary energy balance. Specifically, I am interested in the different spatial and temporal scales of shortwave and longwave radiative anomalies and the physics behind them. Along this road, I try to describe known climate modes of variability like the Pacific Decadal Oscillation using energetic terms.
